Crafting Quality Bags for Over 8 Years

Established in 2018 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, SAZ Enterprises has grown from a local workshop into a prominent manufacturer supplying wholesale and retail buyers. We design and manufacture an extensive catalog featuring school bags, college backpacks, laptop bags, travel duffels, and fancy kids backpacks.

Our core philosophy relies on choosing superior materials and utilizing experienced stitching methods. This ensures that every bag bearing the SAZ Enterprises mark is designed to survive daily heavy loads, rough transit, and changing weather conditions. We operate on bulk business models, allowing us to deliver institutional custom logos and wholesale orders at extremely competitive price ranges.
